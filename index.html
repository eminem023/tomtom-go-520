How to Update TomTom GO 520 Maps

The TomTom GO 520 is a powerful navigation device with Wi-Fi capabilities. Keeping its maps updated ensures accurate routing, updated road changes, and better trip planning. This guide will walk you through the process of updating your TomTom GO 520 maps easily.

Why You Should Update Your GO 520 Maps

  • Get the latest road layouts, traffic changes, and POIs
  • Enhance GPS performance and navigation accuracy
  • Access speed limit updates and traffic zone warnings
  • Maintain route efficiency and travel safety

Update via Built-in Wi-Fi

  • Connect your GO 520 to a stable Wi-Fi network
  • Go to Settings > Updates & New Items
  • Sign in to your TomTom account if prompted
  • Select the available map updates and tap Download
  • Keep your device plugged in during the update

Update Using MyDrive Connect (Optional)

  • Download and install MyDrive Connect on your computer
  • Connect your GO 520 via USB cable
  • Sign in with your TomTom account
  • Wait for available updates to appear
  • Select the map update and click Install
  • Let the process complete before disconnecting

Before You Begin

  • Charge your device or connect it to a power source
  • Ensure internet access (Wi-Fi or PC connection)
  • Backup saved routes or favorites, if necessary
  • Do not interrupt the update once started

Common Issues & Fixes

  • Slow download: Use a stronger Wi-Fi connection
  • Device freezes: Reboot and try again
  • Login failure: Reset your TomTom credentials
  • Update fails: Use MyDrive Connect on desktop as a backup

Conclusion

Updating your TomTom GO 520 regularly ensures reliable navigation, real-time traffic, and optimal route planning. Whether using Wi-Fi or desktop software, the process is simple and essential for a smooth driving experience.
